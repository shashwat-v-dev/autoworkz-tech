Who We Are

About Autoworkz Tech

Autoworkz Tech, established in 1984, is a premium multi-brand car repair and service workshop specialising in luxury and performance vehicles. With over four decades of excellence, we have built our reputation on precision, accuracy, and uncompromising quality standards.

Our 24,000 sq ft facility is one of the largest independent luxury workshops in India, equipped with advanced European diagnostic systems and hydraulic lifts. We provide end-to-end solutions for premium vehicles — from diagnostics to full restoration.

Located near Radisson Hotel, Mahipalpur, New Delhi, our workshop serves discerning vehicle owners across the NCR region who demand nothing less than the best.
